Why a foaming body wash and not a body serum?

A leave on body serum with these actives would be more potent per application. But body serums have an 80% shelf abandonment rate. People buy them, use them four times, and they collect dust. A body wash goes in the shower — you're already there, you're already washing. The most effective routine is the one you don't have to think about.

Can I use this on my face?

No. The surfactant system is designed for body skin, which is thicker and less sensitive. We make a face wash (Clear First) formulated specifically for your face. Use each where it belongs. We know someone will try it anyway. Don't be that person.

Is it safe for sensitive skin?

The salicylic acid is at 0.5% — the lowest effective concentration. The PHA in the triple acid system is specifically the gentlest acid class. ProVit B5 actively supports the barrier. Most people with mildly sensitive skin are fine. If you have eczema, psoriasis, or very reactive skin, ask your dermatologist first. We're a body wash, not medical advice.

250ml — how long does it last?

The foaming pump dispenses pre foamed product, so you use less per wash than a regular liquid body wash. Most people get 6–8 weeks of daily use. At ₹449, that's roughly ₹8–10 per shower. Less than the chai you had this morning.
